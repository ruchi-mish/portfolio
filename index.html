How Calder Trophies & Stanley Cups Correlate

Does having the best rookies mean you win the most championships? This project could be taken further to predict the current winners based on supervised random forest model, but aquiring stats for that project is a whole other can of worms. The hypothesis was that a team that wins more Calders is probably one that has better rosters, and better built teams win Cups!

This is Spotify

I perform EDA on the top 50 songs on Spotify globally to find out what kind of music tops the charts, and explore how tastes differ in each country. The set includes data from countries from countries with limited wifi, and since Spotify requires internet connectivity, it may skew the results. Also note, people in other countries have been known to use VPNs to buy plans from India since they're cheap, for example, so the data may be slightly biased. Still, a fun project!
